Course Features

There is no one who loves pain itself, who seeks after it and wants to have it, simply because it is pain...

Expert instructors

You will learn under some experts in both the German language and in teaching methods. A matter that guarantees you the most outcome and the ultimate value.

Latest verified material

We use the same Goethe-Institut material to make sure you catch up on every new aspect out there, which will bring you closer to Germany itself.

Warm support

Inside our classrooms, you get the ultimate step by step support. And as we are committed to going the extra mile for you, we even provide you with this support on a personal level to create a warm and more interactive environment that can boost the learning process and melt boundaries.

Native speakers

Meet, interact, and speak with native Germans during the coffee breaks or even chat with them online. Either way, you will enjoy an outstanding learning experience.

Educational games

You will enjoy learning with our interactive educational games as we are committed to bringing pleasure into the learning process and this is our promise.

Feedback

Your precious feedback is what craves the way we develop our methods. Your feedback will be collected and considered after each level to make sure your experience is like no other.

Coffee break

We make sure you benefit from every second, so in our break, you will chat and mingle with German native speakers to help you overcome speaking burdens and enhance their speaking abilities.

Suitable prices

For suitable prices, we grant you the maximum value for your money with diverse payment plans and methods.
